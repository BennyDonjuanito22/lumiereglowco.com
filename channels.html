Third-Party and Discovery Surfaces

These are the connected public-facing surfaces that need to stay visually and strategically aligned with the owned site.

Third-Party Rules

  • Instagram Shop and Google surfaces must reuse the same product naming, ingredient order, and image family as the site.
  • Pinterest beauty boards use soft editorial covers instead of loud sale graphics.
  • No marketplace presentation should make the brand look like bargain resale inventory.
